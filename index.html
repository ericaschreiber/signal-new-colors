What is Signal

A training ground for influential visionaries and changemakers.

Signal Institute takes committed leaders deeper and faster into facing and transforming the patterns that hold them back. We help you hear the subtle signals that reveal how to be and what to do — but only if you're willing to be truly challenged.

When you do this work, something fundamental shifts. Your relationship to yourself, to others, to power, and to uncertainty changes forever.

This is not a training for the curious.

  • 01
    Take Responsibility
    Everything in your life happens by you, not to you. Own your experience and watch your impact grow.
  • 02
    Live in Integrity
    Stop leaking energy through half-truths and suppressed emotions. Align your life with a Whole Body Yes.
  • 03
    Love Yourself
    See the truth of who you are beneath your stories. Surrender to the profound relaxation of self-acceptance.
  • 04
    Relax into the Unknown
    Release the illusion of control. Trust the signals from your heart, body, and intuition.

Founder

Diana
Chapman

Diana Chapman is one of the most sought-after advisors and defining voices in the global conscious leadership movement. She founded Signal Institute after exiting the Conscious Leadership Group — which she co-founded — to focus on supporting disruptive visionaries as they design the future.

Her work lives at the intersection of radical honesty, somatic intelligence, and the courage to surrender what you think you know.
