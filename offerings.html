Forum Details

Format
(1) three-day kickoff, (4) two-day retreats, (6) one-hour group coaching calls, weekly learning partner calls
Location
Private retreat center near Santa Cruz, CA. 50 min from SJC · 90 min from SFO
Commitment
One year with August break. Full attendance required for all retreats and group calls.
Member Agreements
Take 100% responsibility for my physical, emotional, mental, and spiritual well-being throughout the Forum.
Stay curious in the face of all challenging feedback, including looking at the opposite of all of my stories.
Commit to giving direct feedback to my peers and facilitators.
Have a 10-minute minimum daily meditation practice.
Meet with forum learning partners each week for a 45-minute call.
Post weekly on the private forum discussion board and comment on others' updates.
Pay in full by the due date. There are no refunds.
