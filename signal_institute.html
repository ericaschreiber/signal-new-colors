What is Signal

A training ground for influential visionaries and changemakers.

Signal Institute takes committed leaders deeper and faster into facing and transforming the patterns that hold them back. We help you hear the subtle signals that reveal how to be and what to do — but only if you're willing to be truly challenged.

When you do this work, something fundamental shifts. Your relationship to yourself, to others, to power, and to uncertainty changes forever.

This is not a training for the curious.

  • 01
    Take Responsibility
    Everything in your life happens by you, not to you. Own your experience and watch your impact grow.
  • 02
    Live in Integrity
    Stop leaking energy through half-truths and suppressed emotions. Align your life with a Whole Body Yes.
  • 03
    Love Yourself
    See the truth of who you are beneath your stories. Surrender to the profound relaxation of self-acceptance.
  • 04
    Relax into the Unknown
    Release the illusion of control. Trust the signals from your heart, body, and intuition.

Our Story

We question
the architecture.

Every leader Signal works with has already succeeded by conventional measures. Most are still running on the same operating system that got them there. Signal doesn't upgrade the software. We question the architecture.

Signal is a training ground for the world's most influential visionary leaders and changemakers. Through coaching, intensives, and forums, we support leaders in loosening rigid ego structures and interrupting reactivity — freeing them to lead through unprecedented uncertainty with energy, clarity, and creativity in service of the whole.

All Signal trainings are held at a secluded 10-acre ranch nestled amongst vineyards in the Santa Cruz Mountains foothills.

Mission

To empower disruptive visionaries to be with what is, to listen deeply to life, and then design.

Forum Details

Format
(1) three-day kickoff, (4) two-day retreats, (6) one-hour group coaching calls, weekly learning partner calls
Location
Private retreat center near Santa Cruz, CA. 50 min from SJC · 90 min from SFO
Commitment
One year with August break. Full attendance required for all retreats and group calls.
Member Agreements
Take 100% responsibility for my physical, emotional, mental, and spiritual well-being throughout the Forum.
Stay curious in the face of all challenging feedback, including looking at the opposite of all of my stories.
Commit to giving direct feedback to my peers and facilitators.
Have a 10-minute minimum daily meditation practice.
Meet with forum learning partners each week for a 45-minute call.
Post weekly on the private forum discussion board and comment on others' updates.
Pay in full by the due date. There are no refunds.
Complete an Enneagram assessment and read The 15 Commitments of Conscious Leadership in its entirety.
