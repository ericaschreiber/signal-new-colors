Our Story

We question
the architecture.

Every leader Signal works with has already succeeded by conventional measures. Most are still running on the same operating system that got them there. Signal doesn't upgrade the software. We question the architecture.

Signal is a training ground for the world's most influential visionary leaders and changemakers. Through coaching, intensives, and forums, we support leaders in loosening rigid ego structures and interrupting reactivity — freeing them to lead through unprecedented uncertainty with energy, clarity, and creativity in service of the whole.

All Signal trainings are held at a secluded 10-acre ranch nestled amongst vineyards in the Santa Cruz Mountains foothills.

To empower disruptive visionaries to be with what is, to listen deeply to life, and then design.

Diana Chapman

Founder & Lead Facilitator

Diana Chapman is one of the most sought-after advisors and defining voices in the global conscious leadership movement. She has advised thousands of CEOs and their executive teams, traveled the globe as a YPO facilitator, and trained many of the top conscious leadership coaches in the industry.

She founded Signal Institute after exiting Conscious Leadership Group, which she co-founded. Her first book, The 15 Commitments of Conscious Leadership, is the top-selling book on conscious leadership worldwide.

As a passionate educator, Diana co-teaches Capital Formation at Stanford University, where she helps students understand that consciousness is capital. She lives in the Santa Cruz Mountains of California at Braveheart Ranch.
